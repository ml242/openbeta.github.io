What are you trying to build?

OpenBeta is an online platform for climbers to share information about climbs such as route location in GPS-coordinates, approach trail maps as well as user commentaries.

Think Wikipedia + OpenStreetMap + Yelp with a climbing focus. MountainProject/SuperTopo that is backed by a not-for-profit entity and 100% Open Source.
